About Me

Hi, I’m Dennis – an SEO & PPC Specialist based in Jyväskylä. I love optimizing websites and ads to rank higher, drive more traffic, and turn clicks into customers.

I build ad campaigns from scratch, refine existing ones, and improve site performance through SEO audits, meta descriptions, and technical optimizations. Every strategy is about making things work better and delivering real results.

My goal is to help you drive traffic, generate leads, and increase sales through Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and SEO. To me, every project is a chance to solve problems, refine campaigns, and make a meaningful impact.

What fascinates me is consumer psychology and it is amazing how small changes in messaging, design, or targeting can influence decisions. Understanding what drives people’s choices keeps me curious and motivated.

Technology excites me too. I love testing new tools, optimizing workflows, and staying ahead in the ever-evolving digital space.

Learning never stops. I’m always improving my technical and soft skills through hands-on projects, research, and experimenting with new ideas.

Outside of work, I’m into history, psychology, football, and fitness. Staying active is a big part of my life, and I share my thoughts on fitness and wellness on Medium.
