Pay-Per-Click (PPC) Advertising (PPC)

Spending money on Google Ads without seeing the results you expected can be frustrating. High costs, low conversions, and irrelevant traffic drain your budget without delivering real business growth.

I help businesses maximize their ad spend, increase conversions, and scale profitably with precision-targeted PPC campaigns.

If you need a fresh campaign setup or an optimization strategy for existing ads, my approach ensures every dollar spent works smarter, not harder.

Here’s how we’ll make your ads work:

  • Google Ads Account Setup & Campaign Structuring to ensure proper configurations, including conversion tracking, keyword match types, and bidding strategies.
  • Advanced Keyword Research & Targeting to bid on high-intent keywords that attract ready-to-convert customers.
  • Audience & Location Targeting to reach the right people at the right time, minimizing wasted ad spend.
  • Constant Optimization & Budget Efficiency to refine bidding, negative keywords, and ad placements, ensuring you don’t waste money on irrelevant clicks.
  • Landing Page & Ad Rank Optimization to improve Quality Score, lower cost-per-click (CPC), and increase conversion rates.
  • Remarketing Strategies to re-engage previous visitors and turn interest into actual conversions.
  • Accurate Google Ads Tracking using Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics for precise performance measurement.
  • A/B Testing for Continuous Improvement to test different ad variations and refine creatives, copy, and calls-to-action.
  • Defining & Tracking KPIs to measure ROI, cost per lead (CPL), click-through rate (CTR), and conversion rates for data-driven decision-making.
