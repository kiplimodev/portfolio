Google PPC Spreadsheet for Denstar Fitness

In this project, I set up a structured PPC campaign and documented it in a detailed spreadsheet to ensure an optimized Google Ads strategy for lead generation.

The campaign successfully drove traffic to my landing page, resulting in 12 lead magnet downloads.

  • Using Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, and SEMrush, I identified high-intent, relevant keywords while analyzing competition and search volume to ensure maximum ROI.
  • I set up a well-organized campaign hierarchy, including location targeting, exclusions, and budget allocation, ensuring the ads reached the right audience efficiently.
  • I categorized keywords into tightly themed ad groups, improving ad relevance and Quality Score while optimizing CPC (cost-per-click).
  • I implemented negative keywords to filter out irrelevant traffic, reducing wasted ad spend and increasing conversion rates.
  • I wrote compelling, conversion-focused ad copy with multiple headlines and descriptions, allowing Google’s algorithm to optimize ad delivery for better engagement and CTR.

  • Integrated tracking & analytics through Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics, enabling conversion tracking, retargeting, and performance monitoring.
